Hummingbird Whisky Sour

Ingredients

  • 1.5 oz. Old Forester 100 Proof
  • 0.75 oz. Old Forester Oleo-Saccharum Syrup
  • 0.75 oz. Lemon Juice
  • 0.5 oz. Apricot Brandy
  • 1 Egg White
  • 3 droppers Old Forester Hummingbird Bitters

Instructions

The Hummingbird Whisky Sour elevates tart citrus to a whole new dimension with notes of cardamom, stone fruit, and a wink of rose.

Combine all ingredients in a shaker tin, shake vigorously without ice. Add ice, shake vigorously. Strain into a coupe glass. Garnish with 3 droppers Old Forester Hummingbird Bitters on top of the foam.
